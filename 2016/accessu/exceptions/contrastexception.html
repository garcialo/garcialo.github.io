Exploring Exceptions in WCAG Success Criteria

Success Criteria with Contrast Exceptions

  • 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum) (AA)
  • 1.4.6 Contrast (Enhanced) (AAA)

Summary of Contrast Success Criteria

The contrast of text, including text in images, and its background needs to meet a certain contrast ratio

Key Terms in Contrast Success Criteria

contrast ratio
Color theory stuff; just use a tool to calculate it.
large scale (text)
Complicated font stuff; just use a tool that also considers size.

Contrast Exceptions

Large Text
Larger text can have a slightly lower contrast ratio
Incidental
  • inactive interactive control text
  • decorative element text
  • invisible element text
  • text in images, where the text isn't important
Logotypes
Logos and brand names; even in images of text.

Examples

  • Random words used to create a background and not for their meaning
  • Person's name on a nametag in a photograph
  • Inactive links, inputs, or controls
  • Previous link in pagination section where the link is inactive
  • Logos for a business, product, etc.
