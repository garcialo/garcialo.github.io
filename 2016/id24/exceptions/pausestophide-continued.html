How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

Exceptions to Pause, Stop, Hide

Any situation that doesn't meet all of the requirements above.

Examples

  • Ad that lasts 5 seconds exactly
  • A 15-second fullpage ad
  • Loading animation as only thing on page
