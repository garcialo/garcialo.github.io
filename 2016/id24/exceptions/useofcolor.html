1.4.1 Use of Color (A)

Summary of Contrast Success Criteria

Don't use color alone to tell the difference between things

Link Underline Exception

Technique G183 allows removal of visual indicator if...

  • the contrast with surrounding text is at least 3 to 1
  • has a visual indicator on focus.
