How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

1.4.2 Audio Control (A)

Summary of Audio Control Success Criteria

Provide a pause/stop button or volume control for audio

Audio Control Exceptions

Audio that doesn't meet the the cases for the Success Criteria to apply

Examples

Audio that is exactly 3 seconds

Audio that is less than 3 seconds

Audio doesn't play automatically
