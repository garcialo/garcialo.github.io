How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

2.2.2 Pause, Stop, Hide (A)

Summary of Pause, Stop, Hide Success Criteria

Applies if...

Moving, blinking, or scrolling and...

  • starts automatically
  • last longer than 5 seconds
  • presented with other content
  • is not essential to the activity

or Auto-updating and...

  • starts automatically
  • presented with other content
  • is not essential to the activity

Key Terms in Pause, Stop, Hide

blinking
Switching back and forth between two visual states to draw attention
