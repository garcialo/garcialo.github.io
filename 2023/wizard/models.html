There are a few ways to look at accessibility

Medical Model of Disability

  • Focuses on the disability; not the person
  • A disability is something "wrong" with the person

Social Model of Disability

  • Is not something wrong with a person
  • Disability is a mismatch between a person's needs and their environment
    • Steps
    • Darkened room
