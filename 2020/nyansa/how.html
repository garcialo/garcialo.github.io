Medical Model of Disability

  • Focuses on the disability; not the person
  • A disability is something "wrong" with the person

Social Model of Disability

  • Focuses on the person
  • A person is disabled by their environment
    • Stairs
    • Darkened room

Language - "pronouns"

  • Person-first: someone with autism/dyslexia or who is blind/deaf
  • Identity-first or Condition-first: a(n) autistic/dyslexic/blind/deaf person
  • Person-centered: whichever the person prefers
