  • General Rule: Don't use color alone to communicate information
  • Affects: people with low vision/color-blindness
  • How to check: view it in grayscale

How do I view in grayscale?

  • Lots of helper tools available online!
  • Windows 10: Settings > Ease of Access > Color Filters > Turn on color filters and select "Grayscale"
  • macOS: System Preferences > Accessibility > Display > select "Use grayscale"
