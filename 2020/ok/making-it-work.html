Start small and make mistakes

  • Start with homepages
  • Start with high traffic / high visibility pages
  • Pilot with a sampling
    • a large group
    • a small group
    • CMS site
    • static site
  • Don't try to do it all at once

Engage with your community

  • Services for Students with Disabilities?
  • Attend events (lunch and learns, etc.)
  • Host events (bring in people with disablities)
  • Stay afterwards, meet people
  • Understand challenges of your users
  • Understand challenges of teams you support
  • Create a formal or informal committee
  • Other similar organizations / institutions

Be accessible and flexible

  • Prioritize help requests
  • Making small improvements is better than no progress
