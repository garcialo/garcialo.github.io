Strategy we used at The University of Texas at Austin

In my opinion, the biggest benefit was maintaining awareness for accessibility

  • organized scans by Educational and Administrative units
  • identified points of contact in each unit
  • established a target score
  • scanned monthly
  • monthly: reported scores to points of contact
    • reach out to teams with low scores
    • reach out to teams whose scores suddenly dropped
    • help raise score before next reporting period
  • every other month: reported scores to unit leadership (Deans, VPs, etc.)
