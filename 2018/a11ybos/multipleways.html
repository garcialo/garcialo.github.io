How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

2.4.5 Multiple Ways (AA)

Summary of Multiple Ways

There needs to be at least two ways to find a page within a set of pages

What are acceptable "ways?"

  • Search
  • Links to all pages on the Home Page
  • Links to all pages on all pages
  • Table of Contents
  • Site Map

Key Terms in Multiple Ways

set of Web pages
entire site or sub-site
process
series of actions where each action is required to complete the activity

Exception to Multiple Ways

  • Checkout processes
  • Sign Up or Registration processes
