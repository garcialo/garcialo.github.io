How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

1.4.4 Resize Text (AA)

Summary of Resize Text

Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content

Exceptions to Resize Text

Captions
similar to the media exception
Images of Text
functionally impossible

Examples

  • Open captions for a video
  • Person's name on a nametag in a photograph
