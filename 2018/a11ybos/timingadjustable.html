How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

2.2.1 Timing Adjustable (A)

Summary of Timing Adjustable

  • Users are allowed to turn off, adjust, or extend time limits on a page
  • Time should not be used to limit when a user can read content or perform an action

Exceptions to Timing Adjustable

  • Real-time Exception: The time limit is required by the event
  • Essential Exception: Extending the time limit would invalidate the activity
  • 20 Hour Exception: The time limit is longer than 20 hours

Examples

  • Auction - Real-time
  • Time limit on purchasing ticket online - Essential
