Sample Use of Enterprise Scanning

Sample Use

Adjust frequencies to work for your organization

  • Monthly - Run scans
  • Monthly - Inform project teams
    • "Scan completed" notification from enterprise tools
  • Monthly - Engage project teams whose scores have dropped
    • Continued awareness (bottom-up approach)
    • Reinforce that scans are the smaller percent of issues
  • Quarterly - Inform site owners/upper management
    • Continued awareness (top-down approach)
    • Accessibility Report for Vice Presidents, Department Heads, Deans, etc.
    • Calculate score for each high-level department/group
    • Include site scores which the group score is based on
