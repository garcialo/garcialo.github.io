HTML 101
Headings

Question: Why are headings important?

Answer: They give information about the page content.

Use Headings As Intended

  • Outline areas of content
  • Nest heading levels properly
    • It's okay to skip a little
  • Don't use headings to look a certain way
  • Don't create headings by styling text
