What are they?

Focus states are for keyboard users what the mouse pointer is for mouse users.

Also called focus indicators or focus styles

Why are they important?

  • Tells the user where they are on the page
  • Focus moves through interactive elements
    • Links
    • Buttons
    • Form Inputs
  • It looks good
