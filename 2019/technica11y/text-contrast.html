Text and images of text have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, with some exceptions:

  • Large text can have a 3:1 contrast
  • "Inactive" UI components
  • Decorative, invisible, and incidental texts are exempt
  • Logos are exempt

Note: No contrast maximums are defined. Reading black on white text can cause pain for some people with dyslexia

Another note: Don't neglect your image alt text

  • Yes, you can style alt text

Text contrast clarifications

  • Having much larger text doesn't mean you can go below 3:1
  • The logo exemption doesn't extend to brand colors
  • Low contrast is often used as an affordance for disabled content
  • The SC says "inactive" but its equivalent to being disabled in HTML or "unavailable for user interaction"
