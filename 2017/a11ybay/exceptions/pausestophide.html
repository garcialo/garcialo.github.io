How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Ignore WCAG

2.2.2 Pause, Stop, Hide (A)

Summary of Pause, Stop, Hide

Anything that moves should have a method to Pause, Stop, or Hide it

Exception to Pause, Stop, Hide

Only Applies if...

Moving, blinking, or scrolling and...

  • starts automatically
  • last longer than 5 seconds
  • presented with other content
  • is not essential to the activity

or Auto-updating and...

  • starts automatically
  • presented with other content
  • is not essential to the activity

Examples

  • Advertisement that lasts 5 seconds or less
  • A 15-second fullpage advertisement
  • Loading animation as only thing on page
